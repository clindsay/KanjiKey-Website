How to use KanjiKey

KanjiKey lets you look up kanji by selecting their simpler components. Every kanji has been broken down into small pieces, and to look up a kanji all you need to do is find a couple pieces that you recognize. With this keyboard and a good dictionary, anything you see you can read.
